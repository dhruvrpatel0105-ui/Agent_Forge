Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions
Do I need prior AI experience to enroll? ↓
Basic Python knowledge is all you need! We start from AI fundamentals and build up to advanced agentic systems step by step. No prior AI or ML experience required.
Is there a refund policy? ↓
Yes! We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy. If you're not satisfied with the course within the first 7 days, we'll refund your full payment.
Do I get lifetime access? ↓
Absolutely! Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to all course materials, future updates, and new content we add to the curriculum.
Is a certificate provided? ↓
Yes! Upon completing the course and all projects, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate from AgentForge that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.
Can I learn at my own pace? ↓
Yes! The course is completely self-paced. The 12-week structure is a recommendation, but you can take as much time as you need. Your access never expires.
